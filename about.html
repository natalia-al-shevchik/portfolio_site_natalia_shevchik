My Story

Hi, I’m Natalia, a Düsseldorf-based UX/UI designer focused on creating accessible, user-centered digital experiences. Skilled in visual design, prototyping, usability testing, and design systems using tools like Figma, Miro, and Canva. I recently completed CareerFoundry’s UI Design Program, where I delivered end-to-end projects across wellness, lifestyle, e-commerce, productivity, and entertainment.

With a background in education, I bring strong collaboration, presentation, and problem-solving skills that help me communicate clearly with both users and cross-functional teams. I am now starting a specialisation in Frontend Development for Designers, bridging design and development to build even stronger digital solutions.

I am fluent in English, German, Spanish, French and Russian, which allows me to thrive in a multi-national environment.
